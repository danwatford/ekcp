East Kent Concertina Players

East Kent Concertina Players (EKCP) is a group welcoming players of all concertina systems at any level of ability.

We meet between 1pm and 5pm on the second Sunday of the month at Blean Village Hall, near Canterbury, Kent.

Fancy having a go solo? We would love to hear it.

Feeling a little shy? Don't worry, we'll play as a group.
